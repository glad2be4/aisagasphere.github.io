Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-09

AiSagaSphere respects user privacy and is committed to responsible handling of limited account and service data.

1. Information We Collect

AiSagaSphere may collect and process limited information necessary to operate the service, including:

  • basic account connection data obtained through authorized TikTok login flows
  • technical logs needed to operate, monitor, and secure the service
  • content metadata necessary to support generation and publishing workflows

2. TikTok Data Use

When a user chooses to connect a TikTok account, AiSagaSphere uses TikTok Login Kit and the TikTok Content Posting API only for the purposes shown in the platform and demonstrated in the review videos:

  • user authentication
  • authorized account connection
  • support for publishing user-authorized content to TikTok

3. Scope Usage

The current TikTok scopes used in this application are:

  • user.info.basic
  • video.upload

4. How We Use Information

AiSagaSphere uses limited collected information to:

  • authenticate authorized users
  • support TikTok account connection
  • enable content generation and publishing workflows
  • maintain service reliability, security, and troubleshooting

5. Data Sharing

AiSagaSphere does not sell personal data. AiSagaSphere does not share personal data except as necessary to operate authorized integrations or as required by law.

6. Data Retention

AiSagaSphere retains only the data reasonably necessary to operate the service, maintain security, and support authorized publishing workflows.

7. Security

AiSagaSphere uses reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect service data and account integration data.

8. User Choices

Users may discontinue use of connected TikTok workflows and may contact AiSagaSphere regarding questions about data handling.

9. Children’s Privacy

AiSagaSphere is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law.
